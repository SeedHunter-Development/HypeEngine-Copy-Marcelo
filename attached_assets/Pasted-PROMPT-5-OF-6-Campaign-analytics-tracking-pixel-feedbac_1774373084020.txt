STEP 6 — CAMPAIGN COMPLETION + DELIVERY SCORING:

Create file: artifacts/hypeengine/lib/analytics/delivery-score.ts

Function: scoreCampaignDelivery(campaignId): Promise<void>

For EACH participating KOL:
a) Scrape their campaign tweet via Apify (likes, retweets, replies, views)
b) Score 6 dimensions (each 0-1):
   - ENGAGEMENT DELIVERY (weight 0.25): actual engagement / expected (from KOL's avgLikes), capped at 1.5x
   - REPLY QUALITY (weight 0.15): LLM analysis of campaign tweet replies
   - CONTENT COMPLIANCE (weight 0.10): LLM check — did tweet match brief?
   - CTR (weight 0.20): clicks / views. 10% CTR = perfect score.
   - CONVERSION RATE (weight 0.20): conversions / unique clicks. 5% = perfect. If goal ≠ "conversion", redistribute to engagement (0.45) and CTR (0.30)
   - CLIENT SATISFACTION (weight 0.10): rating / 5. If no rating, 0.5 at half weight.
c) deliveryScore = weighted sum × 100
d) Save to kol_campaign_scores

Compute campaign-level aggregates in campaign_results.
